Home Gymnastics Program for 5 year olds

During this time, it is very important to stay at home and self quartined so that we all are safe from COVID-19. However, it is important to keep our bodies in shape through these excercises so that when we go back to gymnastics, we will be better than ever! Make sure you do these excercises with parent supervision! Try your best to do these excercises every day and keep washing your hands!

It is very important that you stretch before AND after your workout. Stretch out your wrists, ankles, back, legs, neck, and arms. Also make sure you keep on working on your splits and hold middle, left, and right split for about 1 minute each. Stretching will make sure that you will staty free from injury and make the most out of your workout. Furthermore, this workout is about 20 minutes long. If you want to shorten the workout, feel free to do the excercices for a shorter time or modify the workout. Enjoy!

1) Jogging

Jog in place for about 3 minutes. Try to keep your arms pumping, your feet moving, and your heart rate up.

2) Jumping Jacks

Do jumping jacts for about 3 minutes as well. Remember, stay tight in your form!!

3) Crunches

Sit with your legs bended, your feet on the ground, and your arms by your side. Slowly crunch up and go back down repeatedly. Do this ab excercise for 2 minutes./p>

4) Push up Hold

Stay in a push up position with your shoulders over your hands and your legs extended out behind you.Hold this for 2 minutes. If you want to challenge yourself, try tapping your shoulder while holding this pushup position./p>

5) High Knee Jog

Run in place with high knees for about 3 minutes. To make sure that your knees are high,place your hands in front of you and make sure that your knees are hitting your hands.

6) Squats

All you need to do is bend your knees and go down far enough. Do this excercise for about 2 minutes.

7) 180 Squat Jump

Squat and touch the ground, then jump and turn to the other side and again, squat and touch the grouund. Do this excercise for about 3 minutes.

Now that we have completed the workout, let's do some Gymnastics! Complete the following skills, and if you are not able to do them, feel free to modify the skills.

1) Cartwheels

Do 4 Cartwheels. Make sure that your toes are pointed, your hips are squared, and your arms remain by your ears.

2) Handstand Hold

Hold a handstand on the wall for 5 seconds. Keep your toes pointed, legs tight, and head between your shoulders. Repeat this handstand 3 times.

3) Forward Rolls

Do 2 forward rolls. Remember to stand up with your arms by your ears. Make sure you do this on a mat or carpet and with parental supervision.

4) Backwards Roll

Do 2 backwards rolls. Remember to stand up with your arms by your ears. Make sure you do this on a mat or carpet and with parental supervision.

5) Bridge Hold

Do 2 bridge holds for 5 seconds each. Remember to try to straighten your legs and keep them together.

6) Splits!

Do your right, left and middle split for 1 minute each. Remember to point your tows and square your hips.

Thats it for the gymnastics home program! Try to do this 1-2 times a week and stretch before and after this workout. Make sure you have parental supervision and enjoy!! - Tanvi D.
